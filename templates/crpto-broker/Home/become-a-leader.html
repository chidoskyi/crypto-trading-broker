Who is a Isotradex Leader?

A 'Leader' is a Isotradex user, who shares and allows other users to copy his trading strategy into their account. Each trading action performed in the Leader’s account is copied to all the Investor accounts who follow him/her in the form of a broadcasted signal.

What Does a Leader Do?

Oversee the market and instruments, research news and updates regularly.

Leaders are always researching their preferred products, historical performance and news daily. They need to understand the risks and investment needed before moving forward with their actions.

Interact with Investors and communicate updates regarding trading strategy.

Leaders on our platform are advised to explain their portfolio strategy on their profile and communicate regularly with their Investors regarding their decisions on buy/sell, product & instrument choices.

Broadcast trading signals to the Isotradex Investors.

Successful Leaders are featured on the Isotradex ranking where they get visibility on the website, a place where more and more Investors can easily copy their trading signals.

MetaTrader 4 (MT4) Platform

MT4 is most popular trading platform for all levels offering advanced charting, copy trading, and flexible programming options.

MetaTrader 4 is a popular platform for Leaders to trade a variety of assets. It is the gateway between you and the trading markets. On Isotradex, you can link an external MT4 platform to a Leader account. In this case, the Leader account becomes read-only and any trading activity that takes place in the MT4 account is copied over to the Isotradex account.
