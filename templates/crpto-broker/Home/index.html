What you can copy

Stocks & ETFs

Full-share orders or fractional allocations, instantaneous entry/exit mirroring, price-based T/P and S/L.

Single-Leg Options (Calls & Puts)

Replicate trade by trade: ticker, strike, expiry, premium, quantity, and timestamp.

Multi-Leg Options Strategies

Copy complex structures as a single unit: verticals, iron condors, butterflies, calendars, ratio spreads, etc. We preserve leg ratios and leg timing to reduce legging risk.

Index & ETF Options

Copy trades on SPX, RUT, QQQ, IWM, and other index/ETF option chains where leaders trade macro or index strategies.

LEAPS & Long-dated Contracts

Mirror long-term option plays (LEAPS) with proportional sizing and roll rules.

Short Options & Assignment-Aware Trades

Copy strategies that involve selling premium (naked/covered), with settings to manage assignment risk and automatic hedge/close rules.

Ticker-Filtered Copying

Follow traders by specific tickers (e.g., copy only their TSLA contracts) or exclude names you don’t want.

Contract-Level Filters

Copy only contracts that match your criteria: min open interest, min volume, max bid-ask spread, delta range, IV rank, expiration window, or strike distance from spot.
